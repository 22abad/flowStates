# The Two-Month Shock Wave: Finding Flow in a New Logic
## 1. The Shock of Starting Over
Arriving in Ireland felt like entering an entirely new universe. Every interaction pulled me out of the person I was and into who I needed to become. Transitioning from Law—full of ambiguity—to Computer Science, ruled by strict logic, was a jarring shock.

[ZH]
# 两个月的冲击波：在新的逻辑中找到心流
## 1. 重新开始的震荡
来到爱尔兰，就像进入另一个宇宙。每一次交流、每一条街道都在迫使我重塑自我。从模糊性的法律跃迁到逻辑严谨的计算机科学，是一场震荡。

[EN]
## 2. The Death of the “Genius” Identity
For years, I believed intelligence meant not needing effort. Ireland shattered that illusion.  
**Accepting I am ordinary became the foundation of real growth.**

[ZH]
## 2. “天才”身份的死亡
多年里，我以为聪明意味着不需要努力。爱尔兰击碎了这个幻觉。  
**接受自己的普通，是成长真正的起点。**

[EN]
## 3. Rebirth in a New Environment
A new country erases old expectations and reputation. In that anonymity, I found freedom to rebuild myself from scratch.

[ZH]
## 3. 在新环境中的重生
新的国家清零了旧的期待与名声。匿名性给了我从头重建自我的自由。

[EN]
## 4. The Power of AI and Leverage
AI didn’t reduce the work — it amplified it.  
- Faster feedback loops  
- Sharper correction  
- Rapid iteration  
With it I mastered Java in weeks and finished my first 40‑day LeetCode run.

[ZH]
## 4. AI 与杠杆的力量
AI 并没有减少工作量，而是放大了它。  
- 加速反馈  
- 快速暴露错误  
- 更快迭代  
因此我在数周内掌握 Java，并在 40 天完成第一轮 LeetCode。

[EN]
## 5. The Cornerstone of Family Support
My wife’s quiet competence and constant support form the invisible structure that makes this journey possible.  
**Her faith is fuel; my effort is the promise.**

[ZH]
## 5. 家庭支持的基石
妻子的能力与支持构成了我前进的隐形支柱。  
**她的信任是燃料，而我的努力是承诺。**

[EN]
## 6. Finding Flow Through Public Accountability
To maintain intensity, I built external pressure:
- Sharing my study schedule
- Sharing learning artifacts publicly
- Admitting “I’m slow / a bit thick” to shatter pride  
Pressure became clarity.  
Clarity became flow.

[ZH]
## 6. 在公开承诺中找到心流
为了维持高强度，我构建了外部压力系统：  
- 公开学习计划  
- 分享学习成果  
- 主动承认“我很慢 / 我有点笨”  
压力变成清晰，清晰变成心流。

[EN]
## 7. Becoming Someone New
Flow is not escape — it is transformation scaffolded by discipline. Every problem, every debugging session, every moment of struggle becomes raw material for reinvention.  
I am becoming someone new — disciplined, curious, resilient.  
**This is only the beginning.**

[ZH]
## 7. 成为新的自己
心流不是逃避，而是由自律支撑的转变。每一个问题、每一次调试、每一次挣扎都成为重塑自我的材料。  
我正在成为新的自己——更自律、更好奇、更有韧性。  
**这仅仅是开始。**

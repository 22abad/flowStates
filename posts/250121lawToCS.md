# The Two-Month Shock Wave: Finding Flow in a New Logic

## 1. The Shock of Starting Over
Arriving in Ireland felt like entering an entirely new universe. Every interaction, every sound of the streets, every cultural detail forced me to reshape myself.  
Transitioning from **Law**—a field built on ambiguity and precedent—into **Computer Science**, a space governed by strict logic, was a profound internal shock.

## 2. The Death of the “Genius” Identity
For years, I believed that being smart meant not needing to work hard.  
Ireland shattered that illusion.  
The turning point was accepting something simple:

> **I am ordinary — and that is liberating.**

## 3. Rebirth in a New Environment
No expectations, no reputation, no history.  
Ireland gave me anonymity.  
Anonymity gave me freedom.

## 4. The Power of AI and Leverage
AI didn’t replace effort — it amplified it.

- I mastered Java fundamentals in weeks.
- I completed my first LeetCode pass in **40 days**.

## 5. The Cornerstone of Family Support
None of this would have been possible without my wife.  
Her quiet devotion is the structural support behind everything I’m building.

> **Her faith is fuel. My effort is my promise to her.**

## 6. Finding Flow Through Public Accountability
To maintain intensity, I use external pressure:

- Sharing my study plan publicly
- Telling classmates “I’m slow” to force myself to work harder

Pressure became clarity.  
Clarity became flow.

## 7. Becoming Someone New
Every struggle here becomes material for growth.  
I’m building a new version of myself—disciplined, curious, resilient.

**This is only the beginning.**

# 两个月的冲击波：在新的逻辑中找到心流

## 1. 重新开始的震荡
来到爱尔兰，就像步入一个全新的宇宙。  
每一次交流、每一种声音、每一条街道，都在迫使我重塑自己。  
从依赖模糊性与判例的**法律**，跃迁到以逻辑为绝对准绳的**计算机科学**，是一场彻底的内部震荡。

## 2. “天才”身份的死亡
多年里，我一直以为聪明的人不需要努力。  
这种幻觉在爱尔兰被彻底击碎。  
真正的转折点是接受一个简单的事实：

> **我只是普通人——而成长正从这里开始。**

## 3. 在新环境中的重生
没有声望，没有背景，没有期待。  
爱尔兰给了我匿名性。  
匿名性给了我自由。

## 4. AI 与杠杆的力量
AI 没有替代我的努力——它放大了我的努力。

- 我在数周内掌握 Java 基础。
- **40 天**完成第一次 LeetCode 练习。

## 5. 家庭支持的基石
如果没有妻子，这段旅程根本无法开始。  
她在北京承担了所有责任，让我能在这里重建人生。

> **她的信任是燃料，而我的努力是给予她的承诺。**

## 6. 在公开承诺中找到心流
为了维持强度，我制造外部压力：

- 公开分享学习计划  
- 主动告诉别人“我很慢”，逼迫自己努力到无可退路  

压力变成清晰。  
清晰变成心流。

## 7. 成为新的自己
这里的每一个困难瞬间，都变成成长的原材料。  
我正在塑造一个新的自己——更自律、更好奇、更有韧性。

**这仅仅是开始。**

# Metacognition in Learning: The Self-Experiment

## 1. Iterative Optimization
Lately, I have shifted my perspective from that of a student to that of a researcher conducting a longitudinal study. The subject of this experiment is my own cognitive performance.

Previously, I adhered to the linear correlation that "time input equals knowledge output." I pushed physiological limits—minimizing sleep and relying on stimulants. The result was not efficiency, but systemic failure.
Now, I am adopting an iterative approach. I am testing my cognitive "battery life." Data suggests my peak focus duration is approximately 45 minutes before diminishing returns set in. I have realized that rest is not a cessation of progress, but a necessary maintenance cycle for the neural network.

## 2. From Rote Memorization to Structural Internalization
A key variable in this experiment is information retention.
When initially encountering algorithms like the "Monotonic Queue," I attempted to memorize the syntax line by line. This proved futile, akin to carrying sand in a sieve.
I then pivoted to "Chunking." Instead of memorizing syntax, I internalized the *logical blocks*—clean, maintain, enqueue, record.
The code transformed from a burden into a logical flow. It appears the brain prioritizes narrative structures over raw data.

## 3. Continuous Calibration
I have not yet derived the optimal formula. Variance in daily efficiency remains high.
However, the objective is not immediate perfection, but continuous calibration.
I am observing inputs, monitoring reactions, and analyzing outputs. I am not merely learning Computer Science; I am reverse-engineering the user manual for my own mind.
It is a quiet, solitary process, but it is the foundational work required for long-term growth.

# 学习中的元认知：一场自我实验

## 1. 迭代优化
最近，我的视角从一名学生转变为了一名进行纵向研究的研究员。这项实验的对象是我自己的认知表现。

以前，我坚持“时间投入等于知识产出”的线性相关性。我挑战生理极限——压缩睡眠，依赖兴奋剂。结果不是高效，而是系统性崩溃。
现在，我正在采用一种迭代的方法。我在测试我的认知“续航时间”。数据显示，我的巅峰专注时长大约为 45 分钟，之后收益递减。我意识到，休息不是进步的停止，而是神经网络必要的维护周期。

## 2. 从死记硬背到结构化内化
这个实验中的一个关键变量是信息保留。
初次接触“单调队列”等算法时，我试图一行行背诵语法。这被证明是徒劳的，就像用筛子装沙子。
然后我转向了“分块”（Chunking）。我不背语法，而是内化*逻辑模块*——清理、维护、入队、记录。
代码从负担变成了逻辑流。看来大脑优先考虑叙事结构而非原始数据。

## 3. 持续校准
我尚未推导出最佳公式。每日效率的方差仍然很大。
然而，目标不是立即完美，而是持续校准。
我在观察输入，监控反应，并分析输出。我不仅仅是在学计算机科学；我是在逆向工程我自己大脑的用户手册。
这是一个安静、孤独的过程，但它是长期成长所需的基础工作。

# Sustaining the Flow State

## 1. Prioritizing Mental State

I discussed housing options near the university with my wife today. Despite finding a suitable property, I decided to postpone the move. My priority at this stage is not physical comfort, but the maintenance of my current mental state.

My primary concern is not financial constraints, but the potential loss of this "learning momentum."
I recognize this state—a rare zone of hyper-focus.
I experienced it before the Bar Exam (completed in two weeks).
I experienced it before the Gaokao (mastered in two months).
However, historically, this focus has been ephemeral. Once the external pressure (the exam) was removed, the discipline evaporated.

[END]

[ZH]

# 维持心流状态

## 1. 优先考虑精神状态

今天我和妻子讨论了学校附近的租房选择。尽管找到了一处合适的房产，我还是决定推迟搬家。现阶段我的首要任务不是身体上的舒适，而是维持我目前的精神状态。

我主要担心的不是经济拮据，而是这种“学习势头”的潜在丧失。
我认得这种状态——一种罕见的极度专注领域。
我在法考前体验过（两周完成）。
我在高考前体验过（两个月掌握）。
然而，从历史上看，这种专注是短暂的。一旦外部压力（考试）消除，自律也就随之蒸发。
[END]

[EN]

## 2. The Challenge of Consistency

During those previous sprints, I often pondered a hypothetical:
_"If I could sustain this level of efficiency indefinitely, what would be the cumulative effect?"_

Previously, the answer was: _"Impossible."_
It felt like a temporary performance boost—borrowed, not owned.
This is why I value this opportunity in Ireland so highly. This time, the dynamic is different. I am not merely sprinting for an exam; I am fundamentally restructuring my operating system.

I harbor a fear that if I pause, this momentum will dissipate. Thus, I persist—not out of inherent strength, but out of a refusal to regress to mediocrity.

[END]

[ZH]

## 2. 一致性的挑战

在之前的那些冲刺中，我经常思考一个假设：
_“如果我能无限期地维持这种效率水平，会有什么样的累积效应？”_

以前，答案是：_“不可能。”_
感觉这就像是临时的性能提升——是借来的，不是拥有的。
这就是为什么我如此看重这次在爱尔兰的机会。这一次，动力不同了。我不仅仅是为了考试而冲刺；我是在从根本上重构我的操作系统。

我怀有一种恐惧，如果我停下来，这种势头就会消散。因此，我坚持下去——不是出于与生俱来的力量，而是出于拒绝退回到平庸。
[END]

[EN]

## 3. Support Systems

I communicated to my wife today: _"We have persevered for nearly three months. Another two months is manageable. I believe that in four months, the outcome will be transformative."_

Realistically, this is not a solitary endeavor.
Her support is the cornerstone of my persistence.
She provides the stability that allows me to endure the commute, the late-night coding, and the isolation. I accept the current austerity—the student accommodation, the fatigue—as a necessary investment. I am reserving the rewards for our reunion.

We will achieve our objectives. It is a certainty.

[END]

[ZH]

## 3. 支持系统

今天我向妻子表达了我的想法：_“我们已经坚持了快三个月。再坚持两个月是可控的。我相信四个月后，结果将是变革性的。”_

现实地说，这不是一个人的努力。
她的支持是我坚持的基石。
她提供了稳定性，使我能够忍受通勤、深夜编码和孤独。我接受目前的紧缩——学生宿舍、疲劳——作为必要的投资。我将把回报留到我们团聚的那一刻。

我们将实现我们的目标。这是肯定的。
[END]
